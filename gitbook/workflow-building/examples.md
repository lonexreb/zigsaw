## 📧 **Email & Communication Automation**

### **Smart Email Routing**
**Prompt:**
```
"When emails arrive in support@company.com, analyze the content with AI to determine urgency and department, then route to the appropriate team and send auto-acknowledgment to the customer."
```

**Workflow:**
Email Trigger → AI Content Analysis → Routing Logic → Team Assignment → Customer Reply

**Business Impact:** 75% reduction in manual email sorting, faster response times
