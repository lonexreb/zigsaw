# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## 🚀 **Getting Started**

### **Do I need coding experience to use Zigsaw?**
Absolutely not! Zigsaw is designed as a 100% no-code platform. Simply describe what you want to automate in plain English, and our AI will create the workflow for you.

### **How long does it take to create a workflow?**
Most workflows can be created in under 5 minutes using our natural language interface. Complex enterprise workflows might take 15-30 minutes depending on integrations and requirements.

### **Can I try Zigsaw for free?**
Yes! We offer a free tier with 100 workflow executions per month and access to basic AI models. Perfect for getting started and testing your automation ideas.

## 🤖 **AI and Natural Language**

### **What AI models does Zigsaw support?**
We support Claude 4, GPT-4, Gemini, Groq, and several specialized models. You can specify which model to use or let our AI choose the best one for your task.

### **How accurate is the natural language understanding?**
Our AI has been trained on thousands of automation scenarios and achieves 95%+ accuracy in understanding workflow requirements. If something isn't clear, it will ask clarifying questions.

### **Can I speak in languages other than English?**
Currently, our primary support is for English, but we're expanding to support Spanish, French, German, and other major languages. The visual builder works regardless of language preference.

## 🔗 **Integrations**

### **Which services can I connect to?**
We support 100+ integrations including Gmail, GitHub, Slack, Google Calendar, Salesforce, and many more. We're constantly adding new integrations based on user requests.

### **How do I connect my accounts securely?**
All connections use OAuth2 or secure API keys. We never store your passwords, and all credentials are encrypted. You maintain full control over your data.

### **Can I connect custom APIs?**
Yes! Our API connector nodes allow you to connect to any REST API with custom headers, authentication, and data transformation.

## ⚡ **Workflow Execution**

### **How fast do workflows execute?**
Most workflows execute in under 200ms. Complex workflows with multiple AI calls may take 5-30 seconds depending on the AI models and data processing required.

### **Can workflows run automatically?**
Yes! You can set up triggers like webhooks, schedules, file uploads, or external events to run workflows automatically without manual intervention.

### **What happens if a workflow fails?**
We have built-in error handling with automatic retries, detailed error logs, and notification systems. You can configure custom error handling for mission-critical workflows.

## 💰 **Pricing and Billing**

### **How does pricing work?**
We charge based on workflow executions and AI model usage. Our free tier includes 100 executions/month, Pro plan offers 10,000 executions for $29/month, and Enterprise has custom pricing.

### **Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?**
Yes! We offer 50% discounts for verified nonprofit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our support team for qualification details.

### **Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?**
Absolutely! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply at your next billing cycle.

## 🛡️ **Security and Privacy**

### **Is my data secure?**
Yes! All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We're SOC 2 compliant and follow industry best practices for data protection.

### **Do you have access to my workflow data?**
No, we use zero-knowledge architecture. Your workflow data and configurations are encrypted with keys only you control. Our support team cannot access your sensitive data.

### **Are you GDPR compliant?**
Yes, we're fully GDPR compliant with data portability, right to deletion, and privacy by design. You can export or delete all your data at any time.

## 🔧 **Technical Questions**

### **Can I export my workflows?**
Yes! You can export workflows in JSON format for backup, sharing, or migration. You can also duplicate workflows within your account.

### **Do you have an API?**
Yes! Our REST API allows you to create, modify, and execute workflows programmatically. Perfect for enterprise integrations and custom applications.

### **Can workflows call other workflows?**
Absolutely! You can create modular workflows that call sub-workflows, enabling complex automation architectures and reusable components.
